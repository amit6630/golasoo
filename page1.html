01.

Role

A graphic designer is a professional who combines art and technology to create visually appealing and communicative designs.

02.

Qualifications

While formal education is not always a requirement, most graphic designers hold a degree or diploma in graphic design or a related field. Additionally, a strong portfolio showcasing design skills and creativity is crucial.

03.

Software

Graphic designers use a variety of software tools to create their designs. Adobe Creative Cloud is widely used in the industry, including programs like Photoshop for image editing, Illustrator for vector graphics, and InDesign for layout design.

04.

New Project

When starting a new design project, graphic designers typically begin by understanding the client's requirements, goals, and target audience. They conduct research, gather relevant information, and create a design brief.
